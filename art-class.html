Art & Crafts

At Ikigai, our Art and Craft program is designed to inspire creativity and hands-on learning. Through a variety of mediums and techniques, students explore drawing, painting, sculpture, and more, allowing them to develop artistic skills and express their unique ideas.

Curriculum Overview

Elementary School

Basic Drawing & Painting

Introduction to color theory, shapes, and textures using crayons, watercolors, and markers.

Craft Projects

Simple crafts using paper, clay, and other materials to create imaginative projects.

Collage & Mixed Media

Encouraging creativity by combining various materials to make artwork.

Middle School

Advanced Drawing & Painting

Learning shading, perspective, and proportion using graphite, pastels, and acrylics.

3D Art & Sculpture

Introduction to working with clay, wire, and other materials to create three-dimensional art.

Cultural Art Exploration

Exploring art from various cultures and historical periods to inspire studentsâ€™ own creations.

High School

Art History & Criticism

Studying art movements, styles, and critical analysis to inform their own artistic growth.

Portfolio Development

Creating a professional art portfolio showcasing their best work for college applications or exhibitions.

Exhibitions & Competitions

Opportunities for students to display their artwork in school and local exhibitions, as well as participate in art competitions to gain real-world experience.
