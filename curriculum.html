Core Academics

Mathematics : Our mathematics program emphasizes problem-solving, analytical thinking, and real-world application. Students progress through a curriculum that builds a strong foundation in arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and calculus, preparing them for advanced studies and everyday life.

Science : At Ikigai School, we offer a dynamic and engaging science curriculum designed to ignite curiosity and foster a deep understanding of the natural world. Our program emphasizes hands-on learning and real-world applications, allowing students to explore scientific concepts through experiments, investigations, and interactive projects.

Social Studies : Our social studies curriculum covers history, geography, economics, and civics. Students learn about the complexities of societies, cultures, and governments, developing a global perspective and an understanding of their role as informed citizens.

World Languages

Language Offerings : Language Offerings : Ikigai offers instruction in multiple world languages, including Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Japanese. Learning a second language helps students develop cognitive flexibility, cultural awareness, and communication skills.

Cultural Immersion : Beyond language instruction, students are exposed to the cultures associated with the languages they study. This includes cultural events, exchange programs, and interaction with native speakers, helping students to gain a deeper understanding of the global community.

Arts and Humanities

Visual Arts : Our visual arts program encourages creativity and self-expression through drawing, painting, sculpture, and digital art. Students learn about art history and contemporary practices, developing both technical skills and artistic vision.

Humanities : Humanities at Ikigai encompasses philosophy, literature, and ethics, allowing students to explore human experience and thought across different cultures and time periods. Critical thinking and discussion are central to this interdisciplinary program

Physical Education and Wellness

Physical Education : Physical education is a vital part of the curriculum at Ikigai. Students engage in a variety of sports and fitness activities that promote physical health, teamwork, and a lifelong appreciation for an active lifestyle.

Health and Wellness: Our health education program covers nutrition, mental health, and personal well-being. Students learn about maintaining a healthy lifestyle, managing stress, and making informed decisions that impact their overall well-being.

Mindfulness Practices : Mindfulness Practices Ikigai incorporates mindfulness and meditation into the curriculum, helping students develop focus, resilience, and emotional intelligence. These practices support mental health and academic success.
