Active Bodies, Healthy Minds

Empowering students through fitness, teamwork, and lifelong wellness.

Movement & Fun

At Ikigai, students are introduced to the joy of physical activity through fun games and basic motor skill development. They learn the importance of staying active and begin to develop coordination, balance, and teamwork.

Curriculum Overview

Elementary School

Fundamental Concepts

Introduction to topics like the water cycle, plant life, simple machines, and basic physics through fun, interactive lessons.

Hands-On Experiments

Simple, engaging experiments that help students explore scientific ideas in a tangible way.

Environmental Awareness

Learning about ecosystems, weather, and sustainability to develop an appreciation for the natural world.

Middle School

Biology Basics

Exploring the human body, cells, genetics, and ecosystems in more depth.

Introduction to Chemistry

Understanding atoms, molecules, and chemical reactions through experiments.

Physics Fundamentals

Learning about forces, energy, and motion through interactive projects.

High School

Advanced Biology

Studying genetics, microbiology, anatomy, and ecological systems in greater detail.

Chemistry & Lab Techniques

Exploring organic and inorganic chemistry, mastering lab safety, and conducting complex chemical experiments

Physics & Engineering

nderstanding advanced topics like electricity, magnetism, thermodynamics, and quantum mechanics through practical application.
